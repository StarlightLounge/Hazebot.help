Getting Started

Welcome to the Elite Elysium protocol. Haze Bot is designed to be the ultimate companion for your Discord community, blending advanced AI with a deeply engaging social economy.

1-Click Infrastructure

To initialize the bot in your server, use one of our specialized transformation commands. This will autonomously build your community's architecture.

/setup [template] [mode] Deploy a professional architecture. Templates: **Elite Elysium**, **The Chronic Crypt**, **The Vibe Lounge**, **Afterdark 18+**.
/setup_crypt Shortcut for a total redo of your server into The Chronic Crypt template.

The Exchange & Boutique

The core of our digital civilization. Users harvest Influence (H$), manage their stash, and purchase high-fidelity assets.

/daily Harvest your daily Influence stash. Premium nodes receive 2x rewards.
/shop [GUI] Browse the Boutique for glassware, sentient seeds, and elite status badges.
/inventory Inspect your current digital stash and secured assets.

The Gaming Sector

Risk your Influence in our high-stakes gambling modules to ascend the leaderboard faster.

/blackjack [amount] [Interactive] Duel the dealer in a high-fidelity card game.
/roulette [amount] [color]

Sovereign Soundstage

High-fidelity audio streaming with real-time HUD control and community playlist synchronization.

/play [query] Synchronize the Soundstage with a YouTube/SoundCloud frequency.
/queue Display the currently synchronizing neural audio sequence.
