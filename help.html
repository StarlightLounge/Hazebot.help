Getting Started

Welcome to the Elite Elysium protocol. Haze Bot is designed to be the ultimate companion for your Discord community, blending advanced AI with a deeply engaging social economy.

Quick Setup

To initialize the bot in your server, ensure you have the Administrator permission and use the following command:

/setup_elysium The definitive server transformation command. Purges existing channels/roles and rebuilds the Elysium infrastructure.

Alternatively, for a focused support environment:

/setup_support Deploys a professional Support Hub with a live ticket system and staff roles.

The Exchange

The Exchange is the heart of the Haze Bot economy. Users can harvest Influence, trade rare seeds, and manage their status within the community.

/harvest Gather Influence from your current cultivation.
/boutique Browse the exclusive shop for rare seeds, roles, and upgrades.
/trade Exchange Influence or items with other members of the Elysium.

Sovereign AI Concierge

Powered by neural network protocols, the Sovereign Concierge is your direct link to the bot's intelligence.

/plug [thought] Consult with the AI. Ask questions, get vibe checks, or automate complex server tasks.

The AI also performs automatic "Vibe Checks" in your DMs when you first join the server to ensure frequency alignment.

Astral Ascension

Our metabolic leveling system rewards quality interactions. As you participate in the community, you earn XP and ascend through tiered prestige roles.

  • Tier 1-10: New Bud & Citizen Stoner
  • Tier 30+: Elite Smoker
  • Tier 100: Eternal O.G.
